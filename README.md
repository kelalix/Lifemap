# Life in Weeks Tracker

A visual **life-in-weeks tracker** that lets you see each week of your life as a box, track your progress, and remember which weeks you’ve completed. Inspired by the famous “Life Calendar” concept, this tracker helps you reflect on time and make every week count.

---

## Features

- **100-year view**: 5200 weeks displayed in a grid.  
- **Age group colors**: Different background colors for life stages:
  - 0–20 years → blue  
  - 21–40 years → green  
  - 41–60 years → yellow  
  - 61–80 years → orange  
  - 81–100 years → red  
- **Click to mark**: Click any week to mark it as completed.  
- **Progress saved**: Your checked weeks are saved automatically in the browser.  
- **Dynamic background**: The page darkens gradually as more weeks are checked.  
- **Life stats**: Displays average human life expectancy, total weeks, and checked weeks count.
